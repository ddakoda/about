Why code?

The beauty of becoming a developer is that there is no one path to follow.

From the time I was a kid, I was always trying to find another way to solve a problem.

I was never compelled to do things the"right way". I always wanted to find the "better way".

In development, there is often a better way to solve a problem.

A more efficient way. A more clever way.

For a person like me, this opportunity is unprecedented and keeps me coming back every day to keep learning and solving.
